Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InstaSnap

Getting Started

How do I download an Instagram Reel?

It's simple: Copy the Instagram Reel link from your browser or app, paste it in the URL field on our homepage, and click Download Now. Your video will be saved as an MP4 file to your device.

Is InstaSnap free to use?

Yes, InstaSnap is completely free to use. There are no subscriptions, no hidden charges, and no premium tiers.

What types of content can I download?

You can download:
• Instagram Reels
• Instagram Video Posts
• Instagram Stories (if accessible)
• IGTV Videos

Cookies & Authentication

Why do I need to upload cookies?

Instagram requires authentication to serve video data to any requesting tool. Your browser cookies act like your login session. Without them, Instagram returns an empty response and the download fails. InstaSnap stores your cookies locally — they are never sent to third-party servers.

How do I export my Instagram cookies?

1. Install the "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" extension in Chrome or Edge.
2. Log in to Instagram in that browser.
3. Click the extension icon → Export cookies for instagram.com.
4. Upload the saved cookies.txt file using the cookie panel on the homepage.

Are my cookies stored securely?

Yes. Your cookies.txt file is stored only on your local machine (in the app's backend folder). InstaSnap never transmits your cookies to any external server.

Video Quality & Files

What video quality does InstaSnap download?

InstaSnap fetches the highest quality MP4 available from Instagram — typically Full HD (1080p). The actual quality depends on what quality the original creator uploaded.

Does InstaSnap add a watermark?

No. InstaSnap downloads the original file exactly as it exists on Instagram, without adding any watermark.

How long are files stored on the server?

Downloaded files are automatically deleted from the server's temporary folder after 10 minutes. We do not keep any long-term copies of downloaded content.

Troubleshooting

I'm getting a download error. What should I do?

• Make sure you have uploaded a valid cookies.txt file from a browser where you are actively logged into Instagram.
• Ensure the Instagram URL is correct and the post is accessible.
• If your session expired, re-export your cookies and re-upload them.
• Still having trouble? Contact us.

Can I download private Instagram videos?

If you follow the private account (and the account has approved you), you can download their videos — as long as you upload cookies from a browser where your account has that access.

Does InstaSnap work on mobile?

InstaSnap is fully responsive and works in mobile browsers. However, cookie export is easiest on a desktop browser.
