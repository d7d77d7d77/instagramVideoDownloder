About InstaSnap

The simplest way to download Instagram Reels and Videos

Our Mission

InstaSnap was built with one goal: to make saving Instagram videos and Reels simple, fast, and completely free. We believe that people should be able to download content they love without paying for expensive subscriptions or struggling with complex tools.

What is InstaSnap?

InstaSnap is a free Instagram video downloader that lets you save Instagram Reels, videos, and stories directly to your device in Full HD quality. It works by using the yt-dlp engine — a powerful, community-maintained media extraction library.

  • ✅ Download Instagram Reels in HD (MP4)
  • ✅ Save Instagram Videos without watermark
  • ✅ Works with public and private (logged-in) posts
  • ✅ 100% free — no premium tier, no hidden costs
  • ✅ Open and transparent — not affiliated with Instagram/Meta

How Block-Proof Technology Works

Instagram implements anti-scraping measures to protect its content. InstaSnap applies several techniques to minimize disruptions:

User-Agent Spoofing — requests are sent with a real browser identity so Instagram treats them like normal browsing.

Cookie Authentication — by uploading your own session cookies, InstaSnap can authenticate just like you would in a browser.

Request Pacing — downloads are spaced with natural delays to avoid triggering rate-limit bans.

Rate Limiting — our server-side rate limiter ensures responsible usage patterns.

Privacy & Transparency

We take privacy seriously. InstaSnap does not store your cookies, URLs, or downloaded video files beyond what is necessary for the current download. Files are automatically deleted from our temporary storage after 10 minutes. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
