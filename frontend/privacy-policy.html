Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

InstaSnap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Instagram video downloader service at https://instasnap.app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Cookies File: If you choose to upload a cookies.txt file for Instagram authentication, this file is stored locally on your machine within the InstaSnap application folder. It is used solely to authenticate download requests on your behalf.

Contact Form: If you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Downloaded video files (files are deleted automatically after 10 minutes)
  • IP addresses beyond what is required for server-side rate limiting

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used only to:

  • Process download requests for Instagram Reels and videos
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Protect the service from abuse via rate limiting

4. Data Storage & Retention

Downloaded video files are temporarily stored on the server and automatically deleted within 10 minutes of download completion. Cookies files are stored only in the local file system on your own machine and are not transmitted to any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

InstaSnap uses the open-source yt-dlp library to process download requests. This library communicates directly with Instagram's servers using your provided cookies. We do not share your data with any analytics providers or advertising networks.

6. Children's Privacy

InstaSnap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@instasnap.app.
